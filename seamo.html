SEAMO (Southeast Asia Mathematical Olympiad)

The Southeast Asia Mathematical Olympiad (SEAMO) is an international Math Olympiad competition originated in Singapore and founded by Mr Terry Chew in 2016 in 8 Southeast Asian Countries. Since then, it is growing its popularity around the world. In 2019 it was recognized by 18 countries.

In 2020 total participating countries increased to 22, including students from Brazil, China, New Zealand, and Taiwan students enrolled in SEAMO 2021..

The SEAMO Math Olympiad is held in over 22 countries, among 20,000 students. SEAMO Olympiad has been conducted in India since 2016, offered to both primary and secondary schools.
